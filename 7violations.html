The 7 Violations of Natural Law

  1. Murder: Theft of Life
  2. Rape: Theft of Bodily Sovereignty & Sexual Autonomy
  3. Assault: Theft of Safety
  4. Coercion: Theft of Freewill
  5. Willful Deception: Theft of Truth & Informed Choice
  6. Trespass: Theft of Boundaries & Domain
  7. Property Theft: Theft of Material Resources
